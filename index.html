Join Us for International Day of Women and Girls in Science

International Day for Women and Girls in Science is celebrated every year on February 11th to raise awareness about the underrepresentation of women in science and to promote gender equality in the field of science. This day acknowledges the significant contributions that women and girls have made to science, while also highlighting the challenges they face in pursuing careers in STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math) fields.

Despite the progress made in recent years, women and girls continue to face various barriers to their participation in science, including gender stereotypes, discrimination, lack of representation, and insufficient access to resources and opportunities. It is essential to recognize the valuable contributions of women and girls in science and to promote and support their participation in STEM fields...
